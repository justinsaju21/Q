### **Academic Standards**
Each experiment in this series adheres to a strict high-fidelity standard:
*   **Theory Depth**: Minimum 300-500 words of physics foundation.
*   **Mathematical Rigor**: Step-by-step derivations for every gate and transformation.
*   **Predictive Analysis**: Mandatory hypothesis-measure-compare cycle.
*   **Industrial Linkage**: Direct case studies of companies like IBM, Rigetti, Quantinuum, Aliro, and Qunnect.

---

### **Student Graduation Path**
Upon completion of all 12 labs, a student is considered "Quantum Proficient" and is ready for specialization in Quantum Error Correction (QEC), Quantum Chemistry (VQE), or Advanced Financial Algorithms (QAE).
